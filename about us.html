What is Studio?

Studio is a website designed for creative professionals, providing tools and resources to enhance their projects, showcase portfolios, or collaborate with others.

Who can use Studio?

Studio is ideal for artists, designers, photographers, and anyone working in creative fields who wants to share their work or collaborate on projects.

Is Studio free to use?

Studio offers both free and premium subscription plans. The free plan includes basic features, while the premium plan provides access to advanced tools and more storage.

What file formats are supported for uploads?

Studio supports popular file formats, including JPEG, PNG, PDF, and MP4, for images, documents, and videos.

How do I contact Studio support?

You can contact Studio support through their "Contact Us" page.
